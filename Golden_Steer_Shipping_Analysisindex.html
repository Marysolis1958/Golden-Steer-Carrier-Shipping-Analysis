Key Insight: There is a clear geographic split.

UPS Ground Saver wins Zones 2–5 (shorter distance shipments). It has a structural cost advantage on nearby deliveries due to its competitive base rate structure for close-in zones.

FedEx Ground Economy wins Zones 6–8 (long-haul shipments). At greater distances, FedEx GE's rate curve is flatter — it doesn't escalate as steeply as UPS GS at Zones 6, 7, and 8.
